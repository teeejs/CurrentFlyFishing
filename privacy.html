Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Current ("the App") is a fishing conditions app developed by Terence James Smith. This policy describes how we handle your information.

The Short Version

If you use the App without signing in, your data stays on your device and in your private iCloud account — we collect nothing. If you choose to create a free account (Sign In with Apple or Sign In with Google), your trip logs, favorites, preferences, and subscription status are synced to a private cloud database so they're available across devices and at current.fishing. Account creation is entirely optional. We don't run ads, we don't track you, and we don't sell data.

Information Stored on Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device:

  • Trip logs — dates, locations, conditions, species, flies, notes, and photos you record
  • Preferences — display settings, favorite locations, target species, unit preferences
  • Custom locations — fishing spots you add, including coordinates and names
  • Cached data — weather forecasts, river flow data, and fishing reports are cached locally for offline use

Trip log photos are saved to the app's local storage on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Optional Account & Cloud Sync

If you sign in with Apple or Google, the following data is synced to a private cloud database (Supabase, hosted in the US) so it's available across devices and at current.fishing:

  • Trip logs — all fields except photos (photos remain on-device only)
  • Favorites — your saved locations and any custom gauge or map pin settings
  • Preferences — fishing style, home state, unit preference, water type settings
  • Subscription status — whether your account has an active Pro subscription, so iOS subscribers are recognized on current.fishing and vice versa

Your account is identified by a unique user ID provided by Apple or Google. For Sign In with Apple, Apple may share your name on first sign-in only. You can sign out at any time from Settings in the App, which stops all cloud sync and reverts to local-only storage. To request deletion of your cloud data, contact us at support@current.fishing.

iCloud Sync

For users who have not signed in with Apple or Google, trip logs sync to your private iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. It is stored in your personal iCloud container — we cannot access it. You can disable iCloud sync in your device's Settings. Once you sign in with Apple or Google, Supabase becomes the primary sync layer and CloudKit is no longer used for new data.

Location Data

The App requests location access ("when in use" only) to find nearby rivers and show local fishing conditions. Your location is used on-device to identify nearby water and is sent to weather and flow data services (described below) to retrieve local conditions. We do not store, log, or track your location history.

Face ID / Touch ID

The App offers an optional feature to protect "secret" fishing locations with biometric authentication. Face ID or Touch ID authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. No biometric data is accessed, stored, or transmitted by the App.

Camera & Photos

The App requests camera access to let you add photos to trip logs. Photos are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to any external service.

Third-Party Services

The App contacts the following services to provide fishing conditions data. Only location coordinates or station identifiers are sent — no personal information is transmitted.

  • Apple WeatherKit — weather forecasts and current conditions based on coordinates
  • NOAA National Weather Service — fallback weather data for US locations (public federal API, no authentication)
  • USGS Water Services — real-time river flow and water temperature from federal gauges (public federal API, no authentication)
  • WSC — real-time river flow and water temperature for Canadian waters
  • CEHQ — real-time river flow and water temperature for Quebec
  • NOAA Tides & Currents — tide predictions for coastal locations (public federal API, no authentication)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-generated fishing intelligence (hatch calendars, regulations, fly shop recommendations). Location name and coordinates are sent; no personal data is included. Requests are routed through a private proxy and responses are cached locally.
  • Fly shop RSS feeds — publicly available fishing reports from fly shop websites. Standard read-only HTTP requests with no user data transmitted.
  • Supabase — cloud database for optional account sync (signed-in users only). Stores trip logs, favorites, preferences, and subscription status as described above. Hosted in the United States. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management for purchases made at current.fishing. Stores subscription status for web subscribers. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions

Current Pro subscriptions purchased through the iOS App are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information for iOS purchases. The App checks your subscription status via Apple's StoreKit framework and, for signed-in users, mirrors the result to Supabase so it's recognized on current.fishing.

Current Pro subscriptions purchased through current.fishing are managed by RevenueCat and Stripe. Your subscription status is stored in our Supabase database and used to grant Pro access on both the website and the iOS app when you are signed in.

Analytics & Advertising

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or user tracking. We do not collect usage metrics, device identifiers, or behavioral data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for any purpose beyond what is described in this policy. Signed-in users' data is stored in Supabase solely to enable sync across devices and current.fishing. Unsigned users' data never leaves their device (other than iCloud, which is controlled by Apple).

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Account creation is optional and not required to use the App's core features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact
